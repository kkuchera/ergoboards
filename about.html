About

Searchable database of over 100 quality keyboards.

Why?

Finding the perfect keyboard is not easy, there are a ton of options to choose from. Do I want a split or non-split design? Should it be tended and/or contoured? Cherry, Alps, Topre or buckling spring switches? Once I've figured that out, the question becomes what keyboards fulfil my requirements? That is where ergoboards.com comes in. We have a database of over 100 quality keyboards that can easily be searched with a wide variety of search criteria in order to find the ideal keyboard based on your requirements. We've looked up the interesting features so you don't have to.

What?

A searchable database of ergonomic keyboards. Ergonomic is used in a very broad sense of the word. Basically we take it to mean "any keyboard that offers more comfort than a traditional laptop keyboard". In addition to keyboards that can easily be bought online, the database also includes discontinued and DIY keyboards.

Who?

ergoboards.com is built for keyboard fanatics by keyboard fanatics. We realize that we might be fanatic in a different way. If there is a keyboard you love that you can't find in our database, let us know and we'll add it asap.
